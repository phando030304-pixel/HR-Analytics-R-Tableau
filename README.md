## Overview

This project was completed as part of the Google Advanced Data Analytics Professional Certificate Capstone.

The objective of this project is to analyze employee attrition patterns and identify the factors that contribute to workforce turnover. Through exploratory data analysis, statistical investigation, and business intelligence reporting, the project aims to generate actionable insights that support employee retention and workforce management strategies.

Using R for data preparation and analysis and Tableau for dashboard development, this project follows an end-to-end analytics workflow including data cleaning, feature exploration, visualization, insight generation, and business recommendation development.

The analysis focuses on:

- Employee attrition
- Job satisfaction
- Compensation levels
- Workload patterns
- Promotion history
- Department-level performance

The final deliverables include analytical findings, interactive dashboards, and strategic recommendations designed to help organizations make data-driven human resource decisions.

## Business Task

The objective of this project is to identify key factors associated with employee attrition and provide actionable recommendations that help reduce turnover and improve employee retention.

## Key Questions

1. Which employee groups are most likely to leave the organization?
2. How does job satisfaction relate to employee retention?
3. What role does compensation play in attrition?
4. How do departments differ in turnover rates?
5. What retention strategies can be implemented?

---

## Key Insights

- Employee satisfaction is strongly associated with retention.
- Employees receiving fewer promotion opportunities show higher turnover rates.
- Salary level influences attrition behavior.
- Certain departments experience significantly higher turnover.
- Employee workload patterns may contribute to attrition risk.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Enhance Employee Engagement

Improve satisfaction through employee support and recognition programs.

### 2. Strengthen Career Growth Opportunities

Provide clearer advancement pathways and development plans.

### 3. Optimize Compensation Policies

Review salary competitiveness for high-risk employee groups.

### 4. Use Data-Driven Retention Strategies

Monitor attrition indicators regularly to identify at-risk employees early.
